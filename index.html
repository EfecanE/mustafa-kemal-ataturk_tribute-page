Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

The father of Turks

Here is the life of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, the founder of the Turkish Republic:

  • 1881- He was born in Salonica.
  • 1893- He entered the Military High School and took the name Kemal.
  • 1902- He entered the Military Academy and published a newspaper there.
  • 1905- He graduated from the Military Academy as a captain and was assigned to Damascus to do his internship in the 30th Cavalry Regiment of the 5th Army.
  • 1909- He worked as a Staff Officer of the Movement Army during the March 31 Revolution.
  • 1915- Mustafa Kemal resisted the Allied Powers at Arıburnu.
  • 1916- Mustafa Kemal was promoted to Brigadier General.
  • 1918- Mustafa Kemal was appointed to the Yildirim Armies Group Command.
  • 1919- Mustafa Kemal landed in Samsun.
  • 1920- He opened the Turkish Grand National Assembly in Ankara.
  • 1923- He declared the Republic of Turkey.
  • 1923- Gazi Mustafa Kemal became the first President.
  • 1938- He closed his eyes to life.
